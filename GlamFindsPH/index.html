Colourette

Colourette believes in beauty in all ways, always. They’re all about creating products that are proudly made by Filipinos for Filipinos. Colourette is certified cruelty-free by PETA’s Global Beauty Without Bunnies Program because they believe that cruelty has no place in makeup. Their formulas are also vegan and paraben-free so anyone and everyone can use it.

  • Rosalyn Lleses Romobio
Chu Chu Beauty

After launching in 2022, Chu Chu Beauty has immediately captured the hearts of many Pinay beauty enthusiasts with a variety of fun packaging options, simple and easy to use formulations, and colorful multipurpose items. Chu Chu Beauty is a Filipino brand inspired by K-beauty, however it was created specially with Filipino skin tones and budgetary constraints in mind. Chu Chu Beauty also embodies the wisdom of “beauty that’s all about Chu” as they provide affordable, multipurpose beginner beauty collection sets that are wallet friendly.

  • Vice Ganda
Vice Cosmetics

A popular Filipino beauty brand founded by comedian Vice Ganda in 2017, known for its affordable, high-quality makeup products that promote inclusivity and accessibility for all. The brand's slogan “Ganda for all”, reflects its dedication to making beauty accessible to everyone.
